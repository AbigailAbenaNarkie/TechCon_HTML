History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of professionals, developers, and entrepreneurs from across the globe.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, inspire collaboration, and empower the global tech community by providing a platform for knowledge sharing and networking.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted numerous influential speakers in the tech world:

Dr. Ada Smith

A pioneer in artificial intelligence, Dr. Smith has led breakthroughs in machine learning and ethics in AI.

John Mensah

A Ghanaian software engineer known for his open-source contributions and grassroots tech education projects.

Lisa Cheng

Founder of NextGen Robotics, Lisa is an advocate for women in STEM and has developed life-changing tech solutions for underserved communities.
